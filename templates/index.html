How to get your Instagram data
1

Open Instagram → Settings & Privacy → Accounts Center → Your information and permissions

2

Select Download your information → Download or transfer information

3

Select your Instagram account, choose Some of your information, then check Followers and Following

4

Choose Download to device, set format to JSON, then click Create files

5

Once ready, download the zip, extract it, and upload followers_1.json and following.json below.
